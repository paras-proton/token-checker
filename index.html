How this works

Every new token on Base needs a liquidity pool before anyone can actually buy or sell it. A lot of tokens get deployed and never get one — meaning the price you see anywhere is meaningless, because there's no market behind it.

This scan reads the pool directly: how much money is really sitting in it, how much has traded in the last 24 hours, and how old it is. No wallet connection, no signup, no tracking — just a read of public on-chain data.

It doesn't check for scam contracts, honeypots, or hidden mint functions — only liquidity and trading activity. Always verify a contract separately before trading.
